*Law of Normalization states that a sensory or perceptual norm, involving neither a surplus nor deficit of input data, does not affect goal-oriented behavior or thought.

†Law of Counterbalance states that the discontinuance of one binary state of behavior or thought elicits the other binary state of behavior or thought to the same degree (discontinued progression is regression and vice versa).

Law of Self-transcendence states that all perceptions can be altered via self-transcendental methods such as self-propagation (instead of "perceptual starvation").
